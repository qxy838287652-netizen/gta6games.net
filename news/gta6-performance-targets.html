GTA 6 Performance Targets: RT Performance Modes On Console, Upscalers + Frame Gen On PC

Less guesswork. More consistent 60.

September 21, 2025 0 views 5 min read

TL;DR Settings That Work

  • Console: pick Performance RT for shooters/driving, Quality RT for photomode—enable VRR.
  • PC 1440p–4K: start with DLSS/FSR/XeSS Quality; drop one tier in dense downtown.
  • Frame Gen: avoid for sweaty PvP; fine for story and sightseeing.

Stutter Pitfalls

  • Heavy rain + traffic + explosions = spikes. Preempt by lowering shadows/effects by one step.
  • HDD loads hitch. Move the game to NVMe SSD.
  • TV “motion smoothing” adds smear. Disable MEMC/compensation.
