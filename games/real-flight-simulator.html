How to Play

Z Turn on aircraft lights (optional, may cause lag)
X Lower flaps and slats if available
C Start-up engines and wait for startup sequence
R/V Throttle up engines (hold to increase power)
B Release parking brakes
↓ Rotate once takeoff speed reached (above 150 knots)
M Raise landing gear above 100ft
• Fly through the rings to complete the mission

Controls

A/D Roll (bank left/right)
W/S Pitch (nose up/down)
Q/E Yaw (rudder left/right)
R/F Engine Throttle (Power control)
1 Change weapon
2 Lock target
SPACE Fire weapon
ESC/F2 Main menu
H Hide controls info
